On The Path Of Revenge

Since I am being exiled, what am I left to do but plan my attack? I do not intend to go willingly, but instead to wreak havoc on my way out. With the time left, I will kill the three devils who have wronged me. If I am unable to come up with a good enough plan in time, I will go murder them myself, even if it means I must die in the process.

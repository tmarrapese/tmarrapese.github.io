On The Path Of Exile

Today I, along with my children, was exiled from Corinth after having a conversation with King Creon. He said he was afraid of me, that I would do some irreparable harm to his daughter. Apparently there are rumors I am making threats against the royal family and my husband. I managed to convince him to let me stay for one more day to make plans for how we will survive once outside the city walls.
